Frequently Asked Questions

Everything you need to know about ordering your custom crochet horse

How long does it take to create my custom horse? +

Each custom crochet horse takes approximately 2-3 weeks to complete from the time we receive your order and photos. During busy periods, this may extend to 4 weeks. We'll keep you updated throughout the process!

What information do you need about my horse? +

We'll need clear photos showing your horse's coloring, markings, and any distinctive features. Please include details about mane and tail color, any white markings, and your horse's name. The more information you provide, the more accurate we can make your custom piece!

What size are the finished horses? +

Our standard crochet horses are approximately 8-10 inches tall and 8 inches long. We can also create smaller (6-7 inches) or larger (12-14 inches) versions upon request for an additional fee.

Can you create horses that have passed away? +

Absolutely. We understand how meaningful these memorial pieces can be, and we take extra care when creating crochet horses to honor beloved companions who have passed. These pieces often become treasured keepsakes for families.

What if I'm not completely satisfied with my order? +

Your satisfaction is our priority! We'll work with you during the creation process to ensure accuracy. If you're not happy with the final result, we'll make reasonable adjustments to get it right. We want you to love your custom horse!

Do you ship internationally? +

Currently, we ship within the United States and Canada. International shipping may be available for an additional fee – please contact us to discuss your specific location and shipping needs.
